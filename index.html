The Mission

Resilience is a Porn Blocker as well as a movement to help all people take their lives back from pornography addictions. By building a community of those willing to improve their lives we can support each other in fighting a system that preys on its users and deteriorates the mental health of society.

Why

Research has shown the strain that pornography has on the minds of developing youth and how it is one of the hardest addictions to break. We want people to have control of their lives, because no addiction is a good addiction.

How

You can use this link to download the chrome extension, and if thats not enough there is an app to help you not give up on being the person you want to be.

About the Creator

Ayden Humphries is the creator of Resilience, the apps, and the movement. He has been a porn addict since he was 13 and has been fighting to rebuild his life and not let the addiction warp how he sees the world. He spent years looking for a blocker that would prevent him from allowing the struggle to win over him and eventually decided to make something that would be strong enough to prevent him from getting around it as much as Resilience is for you, it is for him.
